Dos and Don'ts of Earthquake Preparedness

Dos

  • Secure heavy furniture and appliances to prevent them from falling.
  • Create an emergency kit with essential supplies like water, food, and first aid.
  • Identify safe spots in your home and workplace for shelter during earthquakes.
  • Practice "Drop, Cover, and Hold On" during an earthquake.
  • Check for gas leaks, short circuits, and any water pipe leaks
  • Stay informed about earthquake alerts and updates from local authorities.

Don'ts

  • Don't use elevators during an earthquake.
  • Avoid standing near windows, mirrors, or glass objects that could shatter.
  • Don't run outside during shaking as you may be injured by falling debris.
  • Avoid lighting matches or using open flames in case of gas leaks.
  • Don't spread rumors or false information about earthquakes.
  • Don't Neglect to retrofit older buildings if necessary.
